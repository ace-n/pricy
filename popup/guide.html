What is Pricy?

Pricy is a Chrome Extension that adds TF2 item prices to several major trading sites.

Info sources

Pricy currently queries TF2 WH, Trade.tf, and Backpack.tf.

Supported sites

Pricy currently supports TF2 WH, Trade.tf, Bazaar.tf, and TF2 Outpost.

Symbology reference

General information

- price of an item excluding parts/paints

- price of an item, including parts/paints

TF2 WH information

- TF2 WH stock ([current]/[maximum])

- price TF2 WH charges for an item

- price TF2 WH pays for an item

Currency items
